Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Finnwick different from other smart home providers?

Finnwick stands out through our commitment to creating intuitive, user-friendly smart home solutions that truly work together seamlessly. Unlike many competitors, our products are designed with both functionality and aesthetics in mind, ensuring they blend perfectly into your home while providing superior performance.

Do Finnwick devices work with other smart home systems?

Yes! Finnwick products are designed with compatibility in mind. Our devices work with major smart home platforms like Google Home, Amazon Alexa, and Apple HomeKit, allowing for easy integration with your existing smart home ecosystem.

How secure are Finnwick smart home devices?

Security is our top priority. All Finnwick devices utilize end-to-end encryption, regular security updates, and strict data protection policies. We never sell your data and give you complete control over your privacy settings.

What kind of warranty do Finnwick products come with?

All Finnwick products come with a standard 2-year warranty that covers manufacturing defects. We also offer an extended warranty program for additional protection and peace of mind.

How do I set up my Finnwick devices?

Setting up Finnwick devices is simple! Download our mobile app, connect to your home WiFi, and follow the guided setup process. Most devices can be fully configured and ready to use in under 5 minutes.
